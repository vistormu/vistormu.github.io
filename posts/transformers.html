Introduction

As its name indicates, transformers have transformed the field of natural language processing (NLP) in the past few years. The transformer architecture has become the de facto standard for NLP tasks, setting new performance benchmarks on lots of NLP tasks.

The following sections will cover the main concepts behind transformers, from input to output in the same order as data is processed. We will take as an example from now on the following input sentence: 

The quick brown fox jumps over the lazy dog.
